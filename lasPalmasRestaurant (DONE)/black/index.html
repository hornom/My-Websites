In 2016, first generation Dominican Seila Green took control of Roslindale’s Las Palmas, with the goal of providing delicious family-style Dominican food to the people of Boston. Along with her husband, Roberto Green, they have been working hard on giving the restaurant a major facelift by remodeling and enhancing the flavors.

This hidden gem of a restaurant has been met with rave reviews, with many customers commenting on the freshness of the ingredients and the uniqueness of the flavors. Las Palmas is just a quick walk from Roslindale Village or bus ride on the 34/34E/40/50 from Forest Hills.
