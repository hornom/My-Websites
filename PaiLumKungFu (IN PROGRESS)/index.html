When Is Practice?



Practice is on saturdays at 10:am. Make sure to come on time, every minute late is 10 push-ups!

Where is Practice?



Practice is at the Tobin Community Center in the basement.

What is Practiced?



Practice starts with excercise warm-ups, then drills, then form and self-defense technique demonstrations.

Who teaches Practice?



Practice is taught by a number of masters called "Shifus". Click on "The Shifus" Button to meet them!

Please come to practice in black sweatpants and white t-shirts. Anyone with dedication is welcome to join!
